𓁹 KNOW BEFORE YOU GO

FAQ

You must be 18 years or older to attend Lost Kingdoms. Valid government-issued ID is required at entry.

Doors open at 10:00 PM. The event runs until 8:00 AM. We recommend arriving early to experience the full journey.

We encourage creative, themed outfits inspired by ancient civilizations. Think gold, black, linen, jewels, and warrior aesthetics. Comfortable shoes are recommended.

Subject to availability. We strongly recommend purchasing your desired tier in advance as higher tiers sell out quickly.

Tickets are non-refundable but are transferable. You can change the name on your ticket through our app up to 48 hours before the event.

Yes, limited parking is available nearby. We recommend using our executive transport service or rideshare for convenience.

The event is fully cashless. All purchases are made via RFID wristbands linked to your credit card or digital wallet through our app.

Yes, the venue is fully accessible. Please contact us in advance for any specific accessibility needs and we'll ensure your experience is seamless.
